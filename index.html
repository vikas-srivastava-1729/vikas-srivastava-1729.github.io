About Us

The Tribhuj Publishers is a dynamic and forward-thinking academic publishing house dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in Mathematics, Computing, and their applications. Our mission is to provide a high-quality platform for researchers, academicians, and professionals to share groundbreaking discoveries and developments in these fields. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity and excellence by ensuring rigorous peer-review processes and ethical publication practices.

Our flagship journal Transactions on Mathematical Sciences and Computational Engineering is a broad-scoped, peer-reviewed publication covering mainstream branches of pure mathematics, applied mathematics, theoretical computer science, and scientific computing. This journal welcomes a diverse range of article types, including research manuscripts, research letters, position papers, review articles, technical notes, and special issues. Every submission undergoes a stringent review process to ensure its significance, clarity, and contribution to the academic community.

At The Tribhuj, we envision a future where scholarly communication thrives in an open and accessible manner. As part of our long-term commitment to academia, we plan to expand our portfolio by launching more journals across interdisciplinary domains, fostering a global exchange of ideas and research. Our goal is to support innovation and collaboration among researchers by providing a platform that upholds quality, transparency, and scholarly excellence.

We invite researchers, institutions, and academicians to join us in this endeavor as we continue to grow and contribute to the advancement of knowledge through our publishing initiatives.
